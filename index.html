Our Story

Who We Are

Northbest International is a premium apparel manufacturing company based in Guwahati, offering end-to-end production for streetwear and custom clothing. We specialize in low-MOQ, high-quality garments for brands, businesses, and creators across India.

We started in 2017 with a challenge — finding a manufacturer who could deliver premium streetwear without demanding high MOQ. Instead of waiting for the right manufacturer, we became one.

"We became one of the first streetwear-focused manufacturers from the region."

Jubair Hasan

Managing Director, Northbest International

How It Works

From Idea to Delivery

01

Consultation

Share your vision — fabric, fit, print technique, and branding. We advise on what works best for your budget and quantity.

02

Sampling

We produce a physical sample for your approval. Every detail — stitching, print placement, sizing — is confirmed before bulk production.

03

Production

Bulk manufacturing begins with full quality oversight. Regular updates keep you informed at every stage of the production process.

04

Delivery

Finished garments are packed with your custom branding and delivered right to your doorstep, ready to sell.
